### Daily Practices for Energy Alignment and Manifestation

To truly ride the Fire Horse, you need to align your daily energy with its powerful currents. These aren't just 'nice-to-haves'; in my experience, they are non-negotiable for sustained success and well-being. These practices help you stay grounded amidst the intensity and manifest your desires with greater ease.

Consider integrating these into your daily routine:

1.  **Morning Intention Setting:** Start each day by clearly stating your intentions. What do you want to achieve or experience today? Visualise it vividly.
2.  **Movement and Breathwork:** Even 5-10 minutes of stretching, walking, or deep breathing can shift your energy and clear your mind.
3.  **Gratitude Journaling:** Before bed, list 3-5 things you're grateful for. This shifts your focus to abundance and attracts more positivity.
4.  **Mindful Eating:** Pay attention to your food – its taste, texture, and how it nourishes you. This grounds you in the present moment.
5.  **Affirmations:** Use positive affirmations that resonate with your goals (e.g., "I am a powerful creator," "I attract abundance and love"). Repeat them throughout the day.
6.  **Visualization:** Regularly spend time visualizing your desired outcomes in vivid detail. Feel the emotions associated with achieving your goals.
